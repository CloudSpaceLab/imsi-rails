The operating promise

Every bank gets one reliability layer across all IMTO providers.

See failures early

Track provider uptime, callback lag, bank posting failures, time-to-credit, settlement delays, and stuck transactions from one calm control room.

Route with evidence

Eligibility, cost, FX quality, latency, compliance status, and rail health are scored before traffic moves.

Switch safely

Provider toggles, traffic splits, fallback lists, circuit breakers, shadow policies, approval flows, and rollback are designed for real incidents.

Best provider wins

Providers compete on measured performance, not promises.

The bank sets policy. imsi-rails measures live outcomes and routes eligible traffic to the provider with the strongest blend of reliability, speed, cost, FX quality, and operational confidence.
